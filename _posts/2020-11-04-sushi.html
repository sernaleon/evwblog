Ingredients for 2 humans:

  • 250g sushi rice
  • 5 Nori leaves
  • A bamboo rolling mat (recommended but optional, you can also use some baking paper instead)

For the filling I'll use this but feel free to make it your way:

  • 1 carrot
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bell pepper

Final touch:

  • Sesame seeds
  • Soya sauce
  • Wasabi paste

Don't feel like sushi? No problem! You can always follow the streaming while cooking whatever you want. See you there!
